📋 FinFlow — Guide
✕

🔐 Role-Based Access

  • Admin — Can view, add, edit, and delete transactions. Full dashboard access.
  • Viewer — Read-only mode. Can filter and explore data but cannot modify transactions.

Switch roles using the dropdown in the sidebar footer.

📊 Dashboard

  • Summary cards show Total Balance, Income, Expenses, and Net Savings.
  • Balance Trend (line chart) shows cumulative balance over 6 months.
  • Donut chart breaks down spending by category.
  • Bar chart compares income vs. expenses month-over-month.

⇄ Transactions

  • Filter by type, category, or search by keyword.
  • Sort by clicking column headers or using the sort dropdown.
  • Admin: use ✏️ to edit or 🗑 to delete any transaction.
  • Export your filtered transactions as CSV or JSON.

◎ Insights

  • Automatically generated insights from your spending patterns.
  • Budget utilization bars show how close each category is to its monthly limit.
  • Month-over-Month table compares income, expenses, savings, and rate.

💾 Data Persistence

All your transactions are saved to localStorage and will persist across page refreshes. Use the export buttons to back up your data.
